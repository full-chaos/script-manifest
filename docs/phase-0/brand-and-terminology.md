# Brand and Terminology Policy (v0)

## Product Name

- Primary product name: **Script Manifest**.
- Short internal codename: `manifest`.

## Trademark-Safe Language

Do not use the following Coverfly trademarked product names in UI labels:
- "Coverfly Rank"
- "Red List"
- "CoverflyX"

Use replacements:
- Rank Score: **Manifest Score**
- Public Leaderboard: **Spotlight List**
- Peer Exchange: **Script Exchange**

## Naming Constraints

- Never imply legal continuity with Coverfly.
- Do not market as "official replacement" or "new Coverfly".
- Use comparative language only in factual, non-affiliation terms.
